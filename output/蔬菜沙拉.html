蔬菜沙拉

Vegetable Salad

阶段：小学 单词数：30
英文正文
A refreshing dish made from a variety of fresh vegetables, often mixed with a light dressing, and served as a side dish or a light meal. Vegetable salads are a great way to incorporate more vegetables into your diet and can be customized to suit personal tastes and dietary needs.
中文翻译
一道由多种新鲜蔬菜混合而成的提神佳肴，通常搭配轻盈的调味汁，作为配菜或轻食食用。蔬菜沙拉是增加饮食中蔬菜摄入量的好方法，可以根据个人口味和饮食需求进行定制。
